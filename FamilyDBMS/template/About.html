: ABOUT :

A one stop website to manage all the details of your beloved ones.
Don't remember your relative's address?
Don't remember their phone number to invite them for an event close to your heart?
or, are you bad at remembering birthdays and anniversaries?
Let the Family Database take care of that for you
Our Family Database Management System will resolve all of these problems and many more.

Features:
The Database:
This is where you can register a new family member into the database
You can also delete or update the details of registered members
You can also save the personal details like Phone Number and address of registered members
You can retrieve and view all of these details as well

The Event Manager:
This is where you can register a new Event onto the database
You can mention details like venue, date, and title of the event.
You can specify who you would be inviting for the event.
You can then obtain the Phone Number and address of the invitees
You can retrieve and view all of these details in the future as well

The Future:
We will be implementing a Family Tree tracing algorithm to our website
This will give you the path of members responsible for a relationship between any two members of the family
We will also test image recognition features and implement them in the far future
